# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect for differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Constructive criticism given and received gracefully
- Focus on what is best for learners and the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Dismissing questions because they seem "basic"
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that makes participation unwelcoming

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior by opening an issue or contacting a maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and addressed.
